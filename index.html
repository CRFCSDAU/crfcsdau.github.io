We provide statistical to support clinical investigators in and around Cork, at all stages of the research process. We operate under a quality control system suitable for regulated studies, and use open science workflows that promote research integrity.

Education

We regularly offer workshops in the areas of statistics, data, and coding using the R programming language for statistical computing. Please see our schedule of upcoming events here, as well as the Cork R-Users group. We can also deliver bespoke workshops for your research group.

Fee-based services

We also offer fee-based support for existing studies. Specific areas include study design and protocol development, statistical analysis plans, sample size estimation, statistical modelling, and reporting.

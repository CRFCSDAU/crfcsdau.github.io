Open Science @ UCC

The goal of Open Science @ UCC (Oct 31 to Nov 2, 2018) is to motivate and train 30 open science champions, who will work to support open science in their local research environments.

The event will be a mixture of lectures, tutorials, and participant-driven un-conferencing. There will also be plenty of time for small-group discussion and other socializing. Participants will thus leave the workshop as skilled, confident advocates for open science, connected to a network of other open science experts.

Open Science @ UCC will take place in a multi-purpose learning space called the Creative Zone, located in the main Library on the University College Cork campus. The topics and confirmed speakers are listed below. We welcome researchers from all stages of their careers.

To register for the event, please email Dr Darren Dahly (ddahly@ucc.ie). The registration fee is 300 euros per participant for the full three-day event. Payments can be made by invoice or credit card (links for payment will be provided upon registration).

  • FAIR data principles

  • Research data packaging

  • Open access publishing
  • Registered Reports
  • The PRO Initiative

  • Persistent Identifiers
  • Open science for early career researchers
